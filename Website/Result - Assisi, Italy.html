Welcome to Assisi, Italy!

Background Information

Nestled in the heart of the Umbria region in central Italy, Assisi is a picturesque hilltop town renowned for its rich history, stunning architecture, and spiritual significance. Best known as the birthplace of Saint Francis, the town exudes a serene atmosphere, with its narrow cobblestone streets, medieval buildings, and panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.

Cultural Norms

Respect for Religious Sites - As a place of spiritual significance, it's important to dress modestly and refrain from loud behavior. Italian Hospitality - Embrace the warmth and hospitality of Italian culture by greeting locals with a friendly "buongiorno" or "buonasera" and engaging in polite conversation. Appreciation for Art and History - Show appreciation for Assisi's rich artistic heritage by admiring the frescoes, sculptures, and architectural marvels.
