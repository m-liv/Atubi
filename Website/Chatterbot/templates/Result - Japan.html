Welcome to Hakone, Japan!

Background Information

Hakone, situated in the Kanagawa Prefecture of Japan, is a picturesque town celebrated for its natural allure, hot springs, and captivating views of Mount Fuji. Within the Fuji-Hakone-Izu National Park, Hakone offers a tranquil retreat amidst lush forests, pristine lakes, and volcanic mountains, inviting visitors to unwind and explore its scenic landscapes.

Cultural Norms

In Hakone, visitors are encouraged to honor Japanese customs, such as bowing and removing shoes before entering certain places. Politeness and consideration for others in public spaces are valued, alongside a deep reverence for nature, reflected in responsible waste disposal and adherence to designated hiking trails.
