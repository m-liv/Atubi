Welcome to Tsarabanjina Island, Madagascar!

Background Information

Tsarabanjina is renowned for its commitment to environmental conservation and sustainable tourism. The island is relatively small, allowing guests to feel as though they have their own private piece of paradise. The surrounding coral reefs are part of a protected marine area, which helps to preserve the diverse marine life and provides excellent conditions for snorkeling and diving.

Cultural Norms

Madagascar has a diverse cultural tapestry, but Tsarabanjina, being a private island, operates primarily within the context of a luxury resort. However, respect for the environment and local customs is highly valued. When interacting with Malagasy staff or on any visits to nearby communities or Madagascar mainland - Politeness is key in all interactions and beachwear/laid back attire is more appropriate.
