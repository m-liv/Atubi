Cultural Norms

Visitors to Puerto Vallarta are greeted with warm hospitality from the locals, known as Vallartenses, who embrace a laid-back atmosphere and value casual interactions. Respect for Mexican traditions, including greetings and tipping customs, is encouraged, alongside appreciation for local art and craftsmanship.
